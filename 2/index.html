Creators of Universe

Osmanabad district, officially known as Dharashiv district, is an administrative district in the Marathwada region in the Indian state of Maharashtra .

Foremost among the many Hindu gods and goddesses are the Holy Triad of Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva, the creator, sustainer, and destroyer of worlds
